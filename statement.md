## ???? Problem Statement

Most people just don't remember to water their plants in time, which negatively affects their health and growth. BloomBuddy resolves this very common problem by offering a reminder system that's easy: it remembers the watering schedule for your plants and lets you know when they need care.

---
Project Scope
BloomBuddy is a console-based Python application that allows users to:
- Add plants with a custom watering interval.
- Track the last watered date for each plant.

- Receive reminders when the plants are due for watering.

- List all the plants currently stored in the system.

The project demonstrates the syllabus concepts of problem solving, modular programming, data structures, and algorithm design, connecting theory with a practical real-world application.

Target Users

- Home gardeners who want to keep their plants healthy.

- Students learning programming concepts through real-world projects.
- Anyone interested in building simple, useful console applications.
